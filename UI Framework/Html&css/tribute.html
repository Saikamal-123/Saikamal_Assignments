Virat Kohli

(1988-)

"The modern time great."

Virat Kohli, is an Indian cricketer who is considered as one of the greatest to ever play the game of cricket.He holds numerous records in all formats of the game. He is the most favorite to beat every record in world cricket.

  • Born:November 5, 1988, New Delhi
  • Holds the record of scoring fastest 8000,9000,10000 runs in ODI cricket.
  • Most test wins as a captain for India.
  • Most ICC awards by a cricketer.
